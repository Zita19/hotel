Hotel in a Picturesque Setting

Our hotel is located in the Bükk National Park, near Lillafüred, in Miskolc-Alsóhámor.

The hotel consists of two buildings and a separate reception area, surrounded by the soft murmur of the Szinva stream, the fresh air of the Bükk mountains, and the forest.

It’s an ideal place for nature lovers, as there are many nearby attractions and sights, such as the úLillafüred Forest Railway, the Szeleta Cav, the St. Stephen's Dripstone Cave, the Lake Hámori, and the Szinva Stream Waterfal.

The hotel has 3 suites and 22 double rooms.

Rooms can be occupied from 2:00 PM and must be vacated by 10:00 AM.

Breakfast

A plentiful buffet breakfast is served from 7:00 AM to 9:30 AM, and it is included in the room price.

Sauna

The hotel has a Finnish sauna, which guests can use free of charge between 2:00 PM and 10:00 PM. The sauna accommodates 5-6 people, and showers and lockers are available in the lobby area.

Benefits of the Finnish Sauna:
  • Helps the body eliminate waste products
  • Effective in boosting the immune system and regenerating blood vessels
  • Unlike a steam bath, bacteria and viruses are killed rather than produced
  • Improves metabolic processes
  • Increases blood circulation
  • Helps reveal and combat latent illnesses
  • Temperature changes strengthen the skin, invigorate the body and mind, and reinforce the nervous system
  • Essential oils can enhance its healing effects against cold-related illnesses
  • The Finnish sauna causes water loss and accelerates blood circulation
Pool

The outdoor pool, equipped with a filtration system, is open seasonally for guests seeking a refreshing dip. With a diameter of 5 meters and a depth of 1 meter, it provides a delightful experience primarily for children.

Relaxation Garden

Our hotel features a relaxation garden for guests looking to unwind. The garden includes areas for relaxation, conversation, and a playground for children.

Services of Hotel Szeleta:

The prices include the buffet breakfast and other services of the hotel (parking, internet, sauna, salt chamber, seasonal outdoor pool). Bicycles are available for rent at an additional cost: 3,000 HUF/day (from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM); details can be found in the services menu or at the reception.

The accommodation fee does not include the Tourist Tax (IFA), which is 450 HUF/person/night. Those under 18 years old, residents of Miskolc, and families with three or more children are exempt from the IFA.

Rooms can be occupied from 2:00 PM and must be vacated by 10:00 AM. Breakfast is served from 7:00 AM to 9:30 AM.

Children's Discounts:

Children staying in the same room as their parents receive a 100% discount until the age of 4 and a 50% discount on the extra bed price until the age of 12! If one parent is staying with one child, or two parents are staying with two children in two separate rooms, the discount can be provided based on the difference in the rates for the single and double rooms.

By Car

From Budapest, take the M3 motorway exit at the Miskolc southern exit. From there, clear road signs will guide you towards Lillafüred. As you approach Miskolc-Alsóhámor, Hotel Szeleta signs will direct guests along the main road.

By Public Transport

From the Train Station

Arrive at Miskolc Tiszai Railway Station. From the station, take bus number 1 or tram number 1 to the Felső-Majláth stop. Then take bus number 5 or 15 to the Alsóhámor stop. After that, you will find us with about a 100-150 m walk along the road.

From the Bus Station

Arrive at Miskolc Búza Square. Take bus number 1 to the Felső-Majláth stop. Then take bus number 5 or 15 to the Alsóhámor stop. After that, you will find us with about a 100-150 m walk along the road.
